Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

My go-to soft and gooey chocolate chip cookie recipe. Need I say more?

Servings

12, 2-3 Inch Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar (50g)
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed (150g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (150g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 ounces milk or semi sweet chocolate chunks
  • 4 ounces dark chocolate chunks (or your preference)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (180C)

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, salt and butter until a paste is formed with no lumps.

  3. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk.

  4. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula, being careful not to over-mix.
    This will cause the gluten in the four to toughen which will result in cakier cookies.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chunks evenly, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes. For a more intense toffee-like flavor and deeper color,
    chill the dough overnight. The longer the dough rests, the more complex its flavor will become.

  6. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet,
    leaving at least 4 inches of space between cookies and 2 inches from the edges of the pan so that they will spread evenly.

  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until edges have started to barely brown.

  8. Cool completely and enjoy!
